Three Days Passed

by M. Raven Brown

Come, fold yourself into me,
Strong as a shadow,
Soft as a tenterhook,
Beloved cat, three days passed,
Bound of feathers and cobwebs,
Melting away in a sluice of fear,
A confusion of ineptitude;
The last reckoning of my dream.
